Story

The modern burger is often associated with the United States, where it became popularized in the early 20th century. It was during this time that the classic hamburger - a ground beef patty served between two slices of bread - gained widespread popularity, thanks in part to the rise of fast-food chains like McDonald's and Burger King.◼︎



“A chopped steak” is one old school slang name for a burger. This term originates from the fact that a steak was “chopped up” to then combine the meat together and form a burger patty. Even the best cheeseburgers can come by many different names!◼︎
